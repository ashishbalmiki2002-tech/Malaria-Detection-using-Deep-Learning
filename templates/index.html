About Malaria Detection

Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by parasites transmitted through mosquito bites. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment.

Our AI-powered system uses advanced deep learning algorithms to analyze blood cell images and detect malaria parasites with high accuracy, providing rapid results to support medical professionals.

According to WHO, there were approximately 249 million malaria cases worldwide in 2022, resulting in 608,000 deaths.
